Soft Computing

Fuzzy Logic View

A simple explanation of how multiple technical signals can be blended into one overall action.

Membership

A signal does not have to be only true or false. RSI can belong partly to overbought, neutral, or oversold regions.

Rule Example

If RSI is low AND MACD is bullish, then the market has a stronger membership in Buy.

Aggregation

Many indicator outputs are combined together. More buy-like evidence pushes the final signal toward Buy or Strong Buy.

Defuzzified Output

The final label such as Sell, Neutral, or Strong Buy is the crisp decision produced from those blended memberships.
